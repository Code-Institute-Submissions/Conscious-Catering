Catering Capabilities



  • Cheers

    Catering for parties or gatherings of up to 200 people. Buffets, sit-down meals and canapes are all available. Teas, coffees and non-alcoholic beverages can be supplied and crockery and cutlery is available for hire by arrangement.

  • Caravan

    Beautifully converted horsebox into a fully functioning kitchen and cafe trailer that can be parked at your event and keep visitors satisfied with vegan delights day or night.

  • Truck

    Pre-prepared food, suitable for freezing if necessary, can be supplied to stock in your own cafe or shop. Meals, sandwiches, snacks, soups, cakes and deserts are all possible.

Food


Using the finest, freshest locally sourced and largely organic ingredients Henry creates vegan masterpieces to delight all from vegan connoisseurs to normally-committed meat-eaters. The food will leave you feeling fully satisfied yet light and energetic. There is the additional benefit of the knowledge that you are doing a great thing for the planet by choosing plant-based and you can enjoy your food happy that no other living creature was harmed in its production.
Many of the dishes reference global-cuisine and use delicious fragrant spices from around the world. Some examples are Za’tar spiced aubergine stew, Indian hali, Mexican burrito and Tunisian bric. There are also takes on hearty English dishes, such as mushroom, walnut and cranberry wellington or beetroot burgers.
